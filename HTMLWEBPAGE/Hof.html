Faker (Lee Sang-hyeok)

Role: Mid Lane

Widely considered the greatest League of Legends player of all time, Faker has led T1 (formerly SK Telecom T1) to multiple World Championships. Known for his mechanical skill, adaptability, and unshakable composure, he became the face of professional LoL and a living legend in esports history.


Uzi (Jian Zi-Hao)

Role: AD Carry (Bot Lane)

Uzi is one of the most mechanically gifted ADCs to ever play League of Legends. Known for his incredible precision, aggressive playstyle, and dominance in the bot lane, he carried Royal Never Give Up (RNG) to multiple championships and became a symbol of dedication and perseverance in the LPL scene.
